Radar Scope

Displays real-time aircraft positions from ADS-B data over a selectable map. The rotating sweep line and green beam simulate a traditional radar display. Click any aircraft blip (triangle) to see detailed flight information. Trails behind each aircraft show its recent path, and heading lines project forward to indicate direction of travel.

Map Settings

Theme — Switch between Green Scope, Light, and Dark color schemes.
Map Source — VFR charts (sectional aeronautical) or standard OpenStreetMap.
Weather Radar — Toggle NEXRAD precipitation overlay on the map.
Display Airports — Show/hide airport labels. Hover for METAR & TAF weather reports.
Map Opacity — Adjust map layer transparency over the radar background.

Collision Detection

Sets proximity warning thresholds. Aircraft within the specified lateral (NM) and vertical (FT) distance of each other are highlighted in red on the scope and in the target list.

Filters

Max Altitude — Hides aircraft above this flight level.
Min Speed — Hides aircraft below this ground speed.
Squawk Prefix — Filters by squawk code, registration (N-number), or callsign prefix.

Font Settings

Customize the aircraft label font: family, size, style (Bold/Italic/Normal), and color. Changes apply to all scope labels, airport ICAOs, and the center label.

Target List

The right sidebar lists all aircraft sorted by distance from the center point. Columns show callsign, distance (NM), bearing, altitude, speed, and heading. Click a row to select and highlight that aircraft on the scope.

LiveATC

When an airport is selected or the center is set to a towered airport, LiveATC audio feeds appear. Click any feed button to stream live ATC communications. The header shows the ICAO code followed by “Audio”.

Weather

With Display Airports enabled, hover any airport label to see current METAR conditions and TAF forecast. Weather data is fetched from AviationWeather.gov.

Keyboard & Mouse

Drag — Click and drag the scope to pan the view.
Ctrl+Scroll / Cmd+Scroll — Zoom in/out on the scope.
Hover near a target blip to preview its callsign, altitude, and speed. To change the center point, enter an ICAO code or city name in the sidebar Center field and click Go. Click a row in the target list to select and highlight that aircraft.
